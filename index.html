Frequently asked questions

What is the duration of the online courses?

Each course varies in length, typically ranging from 4 to 12 weeks, depending on the complexity of the subject.

Are the courses self-paced, or do they have a fixed schedule?

We offer both self-paced and instructor-led courses. Self-paced courses allow you to learn at your own speed, while instructor-led courses follow a fixed schedule with live sessions.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion of the course?

Yes, you will receive a certificate of completion after successfully finishing the course, provided all assignments and assessments are completed.

Are the courses self-paced, or do they have a fixed schedule?

We offer both self-paced and instructor-led courses. Self-paced courses allow you to learn at your own speed, while instructor-led courses follow a fixed schedule with live sessions.

What are the prerequisites for enrolling in a course?

Prerequisites vary by course. Some may require prior knowledge of specific programming languages or tools, while others are designed for beginners.

How do I access course materials?

All course materials, including lectures, assignments, and resources, are accessible through our online learning platform. You can log in anytime to review content.

What happens if I miss a live session?

All live sessions are recorded and made available on the course platform, so you can watch them at your convenience.
